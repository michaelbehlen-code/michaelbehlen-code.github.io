Privacy Policy

Light Meter Pro · Effective July 10, 2026

The short version: Light Meter Pro collects no data. Everything the app does happens on your device. Nothing you see, meter, log, or configure is ever sent to us or to anyone else.

Camera

Light Meter Pro uses your iPhone's camera to measure light and to show the live exposure and filter preview. Camera frames are processed in real time on your device and are immediately discarded. If you choose to attach a reference image to a field log — a captured preview or a picture from your photo library — that image is saved only on your device inside the app, is deleted when you delete the log entry or the app, and is never transmitted anywhere.

Location

If you grant location access, the app can attach GPS coordinates, the compass direction you were facing, and a place name (like "Yosemite National Park") to field log entries you choose to save. To look up the place name, coordinates are sent to Apple's geocoding service — the same system Apple Photos uses; Apple's handling is governed by Apple's privacy policy, and we never receive or store anything. Your saved locations live only on your device inside the app. Location access is optional — every feature except log geotagging works without it.

Data storage

Your settings (calibration, display preferences, custom apertures) and your field logs are stored locally on your device. We have no servers, and no copy of your data exists anywhere else. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We never see or store your name, email address, or any payment information. Apple provides us only anonymous, aggregated sales statistics. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your App Store account settings.

Analytics, tracking, and third parties

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. It makes no network connections of its own. We do not — and cannot — sell or share your data, because we never receive it.

Accounts

Light Meter Pro does not require an account. If optional accounts (for example, syncing logs across devices) are introduced in a future version, this policy will be updated first, and any account data will be collected only with your explicit consent.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children, and is rated 4+.

Changes to this policy

If the app's privacy behavior ever changes, this page will be updated and the effective date revised before the change ships.
